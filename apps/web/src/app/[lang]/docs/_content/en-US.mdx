# About Plotwist

**Plotwist** was created to bring everything together in one platform. Unlike other platforms like Letterboxd or MyAnimeList, which focus on just one type of content, Plotwist allows you to manage and rate movies, series, anime, dramas, and much more.

Our goal is simple: to provide a complete and elegant space to organize your lists, track what you’ve watched, and plan what you want to watch — all in one place.

## Free Plan Features

- **Full access to the platform**  
  Explore and use the main tools to manage your entertainment.  

- **Unlimited ratings**  
  Share your opinions on any title, with no restrictions.  

- **Unlimited lists**  
  Organize your content in as many lists as you like.  

- **Content management**  
  Practical tools to keep track of what you’ve watched, are watching, or plan to watch.  

- **Collaborative lists**  
  Create lists with friends and share suggestions interactively.  

- **Recommend content to friends**  
  Send recommendations directly to friends within the platform.  

## Pro Plan Features

> Some features of the **Pro Plan** are under development and will be released soon.

By subscribing to the **Pro Plan**, you unlock advanced features for an even more personalized and comprehensive experience:

- **Personal recommendations based on your history**  
  Discover new content perfectly aligned with your tastes.  

- **Badges and achievements**  
  Unlock exclusive rewards and showcase your progress with unique badges.  

- **Detailed personal statistics**  
  Get complete insights into your consumption habits, such as most-watched genres and total watch time.  

- **Priority access to new features**  
  Be the first to try new platform features.  

## Integration with TMDB

Thanks to the integration with TMDB, **Plotwist** offers a vast catalog of movies, series, and more, with synopses, casts, trailers, and other comprehensive information.

## Future

We aim to transform **Plotwist** into a vibrant community where fans can interact, share, and celebrate their passion for audiovisual entertainment. With exciting new features on the horizon, we are committed to making **Plotwist** the ultimate destination for all movie and series enthusiasts.
